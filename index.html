FAQ

Your Questions? Answered

How long does it take you to make a website?

You can expect to receive the final website that will convert visitors into clients only in 3-6 weeks. The whole process of website creation involves the discovery of the unique problems your business is facing, creation of a strategy, design and finally development, because of which the time this takes may vary.

Are the websites SEO-optimized?

Your websites aren’t just mobile-friendly and user-friendly — they’re also fully optimized for SEO. I handle everything from on-page and technical SEO to schema markup, helping your site appear in Google snippets. This ensures potential clients can easily find your products or services through online searches. I seamlessly integrate SEO into the website development process, delivering a high-quality site with well-crafted, search-engine-optimized content.

How are you different from other designers?

When you hire a designer, you usually get someone who's creative and loves design. But with me, you get someone who's passionate about using design to solve your business problems, not just for the sake of design itself. Leveraging my extensive business knowledge from the Bells University of Technology, we'll work together to identify the challenges holding you back. We'll brainstorm solutions, prioritize them using a problem-solving approach, and by the time we reach the design phase, we'll have already completed 20% of the work, delivering 80% of the results your business needs.

What's your process like?

My process is designed to be smooth, collaborative, and efficient:

  1. Discovery Call
    2. We start with a 20–30 minute call to understand your business challenges, assess your needs, and determine if we’re a good fit.

  2. Strategy Session
    3. Together, we define the best art direction for your brand, prioritize impactful design decisions, and refine your vision for your website.

  3. Decision
    4. Once the contract is signed, I develop complete landing page options (2 days) and craft the complete website design (within 10 days)

  4. Development
    5. Your website is built to be fully functional, mobile-optimized, and integrated with CMS, SEO, Google Analytics, and other essential tools. This phase typically takes 1–3 weeks, depending on project complexity.

  5. Launch
    6. You'll receive a guide for repository and SEO management, along with a month of additional website and brand design support. If desired, I can also help increase your visibility within my online community.

What is the cost of creating a website?

The cost of designing and building a website can vary, typically ranging from $220 to $850, depending on the complexity of your project. Before evaluating whether this investment aligns with your budget, it's essential to understand the potential consequences of choosing a subpar, low-cost website design:

▪ High ongoing maintenance expenses
▪ Dissatisfied employees who won't proudly share your website
▪ Frustrated customers who may leave your site and never return
▪ Significant time and effort wasted, resulting in missed opportunities
▪ Poor visibility on search engines, making it challenging for potential customers to find your site and costly to advertise

Investing in a well-designed website can save you money and reputation in the long run by avoiding these costly pitfalls.

Who purchases the domain?

The domain is typically purchased by the client or the business that owns the website. I can guide you through the process and recommend a reliable domain registrar like GoDaddy. Once the domain is purchased, I’ll need access to the account to configure the DNS settings properly. This includes pointing the domain to your hosting server, setting up email if needed, and ensuring everything works seamlessly with your website. If you’d prefer, I can also handle the purchase on your behalf and transfer the ownership to you afterward.
